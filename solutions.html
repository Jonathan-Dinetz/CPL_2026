Basin for Jes Fan Solo show
A series of heated aluminum basins completed for Jes Fan's solo show at Andrew Kreps. The basins hold boiling soy milk, which slowly coagulates at the surface, yielding sheets of tofu skin.
Check-out counter for Symposium Books
Our check out counter we completed for Symposium Books. The design borrows various structural principles from some of our favorite architectural projects, using a sheet facade as structural curtain wall, reverse thread strut as a central tensioner, and custom built leveling feet.
Brackets machined for armature
Brackets for crt armature, completed for Will Johnson's fall 2024 sound installation at Mass Art. The brackets are milled from blocks of cold rolled steel using a fly cutter and right angle attachment.

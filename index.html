Featured Solutions
A custom check out counter designed and fabricated for Symposium Books in spring 2024. The design borrows various structural principles from some of CPL's favorite architectural projects—using a sheet facade as structural curtain wall, reverse thread strut as a central tensioner, and custom built leveling feet.
A series of heated aluminum basins completed for Jes Fan's fall 2024 solo show at Andrew Kreps. The basins hold boiling soy milk, which coagulates at the surface, yielding sheets of tofu skin.
Brackets for CRT armature, completed for Will Johnson's fall 2024 sound installation at Mass Art. One of CPL's first projects, the brackets are manually milled from blocks of cold rolled steel.
